    Frequently Asked Questions

    Provides quick answers to common queries, helping you find the information regarding Divyabha and the Eco-friendly Idols

    1. What are the Eco-Friendly Idols made of?

    The Idols are made of industry grade Alum which is completely water soluble and sustainable. Quick Tip: As the Idols are water soluble, try to keep the idols away from moisture.

    2. How much time does it take for the Idol to completely dissolve?

    The time of dissolution depends on the size of the Idol and the quantity of water. With an adequate quantity of water, the Idol should take close to 4 hours to completely dissolve

    3. Will the Idol leave any residue after dissolution?

    The Idol will completely dissolve leaving no residue in the water. Furthermore, you can use the water for gardening purposes.

    4. How to place an order for the Ganesha Idols?

    Thank you for your interest in our Ganesha idols! You can contact us directly using the phone numbers provided in the Contact section of our website. Alternatively, feel free to leave your contact details in the form below, and we will be sure to get in touch with you.

    5. Where did the name "Divyabha" come from?

    Since we embrace the sacred essence of God and the positive energy he embodies, we wanted to reflect that in the name. The term "Divyabha" merges two words: "Divya," which means Divine, and "Abha," which signifies Aura.
